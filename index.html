Database FAQ

Who is eligible to participate in Techfest 2026?

+

Any student currently enrolled in an undergraduate, postgraduate, or research program worldwide. School teams are eligible for selected junior arenas. Ensure you carry your student RFID card for physical portal check-ins.

Are individual registrations allowed, or is a team required?

+

Selected tracks like Code Vector support individual runtimes, while Robowars and Aerospace tracks require structured squads of 2 to 6 pilots. You can register individually and form alliances inside our matchmaking lobby.

What hardware and APIs are provided for competitors?

+

For Code Vector, contestants get cloud computing credits, high-speed fiber-node connections, and pre-trained model APIs. Robowars teams receive access to safety pits, welding gear, and 230V battery charging pods.
